🥐 Croissant!

Croissants, those flaky, golden crescents of delight, have a captivating story that transcends borders and tantalizes taste buds. Originating in Vienna, Austria, these buttery pastries gained international fame when they made their way to France in the early 19th century. Their distinctive shape, reminiscent of a victorious Ottoman crescent, adds to their allure. But it’s not just their appearance that makes them special.
